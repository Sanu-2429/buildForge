Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how the Cyber Crime Reporting Portal collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information submitted through the website.

1. Information Collection

We collect the following types of personal data when you submit a report:

  • Name and contact information (if provided)
  • Details of the cybercrime incident
  • Suspect information like mobile numbers, email, UPI IDs
  • Device and IP-related technical data

2. Use of Information

All data submitted is used solely for the investigation of reported cybercrimes by authorized law enforcement agencies.

3. Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your data, including encryption and access controls, to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with any third parties unless required by law or for investigation by competent government authorities.

5. Cookies

This portal may use minimal cookies to maintain session information. No tracking or profiling cookies are used.

6. User Rights

Users have the right to remain anonymous while submitting a complaint. You may also contact the authorities for clarification regarding your report.

7. Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be revised periodically. Any updates will be reflected on this page with immediate effect.

8. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this policy, please contact the Cyber Crime Cell of the Ministry of Home Affairs.
