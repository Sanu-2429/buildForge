Steps to Join as a Cyber Volunteer

The Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) welcomes responsible citizens to help in maintaining cyber hygiene and reporting unlawful content. Follow the steps below to join as a cyber volunteer:

📝 Step-by-Step Process

  • 🔹 Visit the official Cybercrime portal: cybercrime.gov.in
  • 🔹 Scroll down to find the “Cyber Volunteer” section or directly visit: /Volunteer
  • 🔹 Click on “Register as Cyber Volunteer”.
  • 🔹 Choose your role:
    • 🛡 Cyber Volunteer Unlawful Content Flagging
    • 💻 Cyber Awareness Promoter
    • 👨‍🏫 Cyber Expert
  • 🔹 Fill the registration form with your:
    • Name, Address, Email, Phone
    • ID Proof (Aadhaar/PAN)
    • Area of Expertise (for Experts)
  • 🔹 Submit the application. You will receive further communication via email.

📌 Note:

  • All data is confidential and monitored under MHA norms.
  • Volunteers must follow the guidelines strictly and report responsibly.

For more details, visit the official portal or contact the helpline: 1930.
