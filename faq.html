Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the National Cybercrime Reporting Portal?
The portal is an initiative by the Government of India to allow citizens to report cybercrimes online, especially related to women and children.
2. How can I file a cybercrime complaint?
You can file a complaint by selecting the appropriate category, filling in personal and crime details, and submitting relevant evidence.
3. Can I remain anonymous while filing a complaint?
currently the portal requires basic identity details for official and legal follow-up by the concerned authorities.
4. What type of cybercrimes can be reported?
Crimes like financial fraud, online harassment, cyberbullying, email scams, social media crimes, and more can be reported.
5. What happens after I submit my complaint?
Your complaint will be routed to the appropriate law enforcement agency for further investigation and action.
