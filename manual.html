How to Report a Cyber Crime

Follow these simple steps to report cyber crimes on the official National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal:

  1. Visit the Portal: Go to https://cybercrime.gov.in.
  2. Click on "Report Other Cyber Crimes": For financial frauds, online abuse, etc.
  3. Register/Login: Create an account or log in using your mobile number and OTP.
  4. Select Crime Category: Choose from options like Women/Child Related Crimes, Financial Fraud, or Others.
  5. Fill in Complaint Details: Describe the incident, time, date, and attach any screenshots or evidence.
  6. Provide Personal Info: Enter your name, contact number, and state/city details.
  7. Submit the Complaint: Review and confirm all details, then submit.
  8. Get Acknowledgement: You will receive a reference number (acknowledgement) via SMS/email.
  9. Track Your Complaint: Use your reference ID to track status on the same portal.

For emergency assistance, dial the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (available 24x7).

If you face any issues, reach out through our Contact Us page.
